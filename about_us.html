About Our School

Virtute et Labore – Virtue and Hard Work

Our History

Founded in 1967, Mona Heights Primary School has been committed to providing quality education to young learners in Jamaica. We believe in nurturing the whole child academically, socially, and morally.

Mission Statement

To provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment where students are empowered to achieve excellence and develop strong character.

Vision Statement

To be a leading primary institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and community involvement.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Excellence
  • Teamwork
  • Responsibility
